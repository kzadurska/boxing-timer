this is a simple timer app, could be used by a boxer for training.It counts time in intervals

main view:
how many rounds (1/x)
how much time elapsed (00:20)
pause/play button
reset button

settings with example
number of rounds 6
time of each round 120
time of break 30
signal end of round in seconds: 10 -e.g. timer for60 seconds. when it hits 50 seconds, 10 seconds left -warning sound
signal end of break in seconds 10
